Roseville Community Food Drive Details

Donations are being collected Now through February 8, 2026.

All donations will benefit vulnerable community members & Roseville Area Schools.

Drop Off Locations

  • Flameburger - Little Canada
    2526 Rice Street, St. Paul, MN - Google Maps
  • Mi-Sant Kitchen & Bakery
    1881 Highway 36 W, Roseville, MN - Google Maps
  • Parkway Pizza - Roseville
    1909 Lexington Ave N, St. Paul, MN - Google Maps
  • The Cafe Meow - Roseville
    1718A Lexington Ave N, Roseville, MN - Google Maps

Items Needed

Please donate unopened, unexpired items!

  • Staples: Rice, pasta, pasta sauce, flour, sugar, cooking oil
  • Proteins: Can tuna, can chicken, beans, peanut butter
  • Instant: Mac & cheese, ramen, rice cups
  • Breakfast: Oatmeal, cereal, pancake mix, syrup
  • Kid snacks: Microwave popcorn, fruit cups, crackers, applesauce
  • Personal Care: Toiletries, feminine products, hygiene items
  • Infant Care: Diapers, wipes, formula
